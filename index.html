How It Works

Simple, Fast, Proven Workflow

1

Add Inventory in Seconds

Scan or enter the VIN, set the price, mileage, and color. Your vehicle is instantly published on your Savvy Dealer website and Facebook Marketplace. No delays. No duplicate work.

2

Capture & Respond Instantly

When a customer inquires about a vehicle or submits a financing form, the system captures the lead and sends instant SMS notifications to your phone. Speed to lead happens automatically.

3

Track Every Interaction

Every step of the sale is logged: forms, text messages, follow-ups, status changes. You can see the full interaction timeline from first contact to final sale. No more guessing what happened.

4

Sold → Reviews Go Out Automatically

When you click "Mark Sold", the system sends the customer a review request, routes positive reviews to Google, and sends negative feedback privately to you. Protect your reputation automatically.
